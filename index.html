Meri Maati: Connecting People With Nature

We're a community founded by three passionate #GreenSpaceExplorers (Pankaj Grover, Jitin Tanwar & Hemant Garg) dedicated to reconnecting people with nature. We conduct guided nature walks, fostering awareness and appreciation for the environment. Our mission is to inspire sustainable living, protect green spaces, and create unforgettable outdoor experiences. Join us in exploring, preserving, and celebrating the beauty of nature!

  • We organize immersive nature walks, helping individuals reconnect with the environment while exploring forests and biodiversity hotspots.
  • Our programs educate on conservation, sustainability, and eco-friendly practices, encouraging responsible interaction with nature for a greener future.
  • We facilitate guided wildlife spotting experiences, teaching participants about local flora and fauna, their habitats, and the importance of biodiversity preservation.
  • We conduct hands-on workshops on composting, waste management, organic gardening, and sustainable living.
  • We initiate and lead clean-up campaigns in parks, forests, and urban green spaces to reduce environmental pollution.
  • Our afforestation efforts involve planting native trees, restoring green spaces, and engaging communities.
  • We organize eco-friendly trekking adventures, combining outdoor exploration with conservation ethics to foster a deeper connection with nature.
  • We collaborate with schools and businesses to integrate environmental education, team-building activities, and sustainability initiatives.
